Quick answers

Questions buyers ask us first.

Do you charge buyers a fee?

For most builder and developer projects our commission comes from the seller, so you pay nothing extra. For resale deals we tell you the brokerage upfront before any visit.

Can you help with a home loan?

Yes. We coordinate with banks and NBFCs, prepare the document set and follow up on sanction and disbursement so it moves alongside your agreement.

Are site visits really free?

Yes, including pickup within Hyderabad. There is no obligation to buy and no follow-up pressure afterwards.

Do you work with NRI buyers?

Regularly. We handle video walkthroughs, power of attorney guidance and remote registration coordination for buyers outside India.
